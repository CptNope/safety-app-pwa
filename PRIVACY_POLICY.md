## 2. Information We Do Not Collect

**We do not:**
- Collect or store user names, emails, or other identifiers
- Log personal data, location, or browsing history
- Sell or share any user data with third parties
- Use cookies or tracking pixels for marketing purposes

**All app content runs directly in your browser, and all data stays on your device.**

---

## 3. Local Storage

If you install **Dose Doctor** as a Progressive Web App (PWA), your browser may store small files locally.

These files are only used to make the app work offline and are automatically managed by your device.

They contain **no personal information** and can be deleted anytime by clearing your browser cache or uninstalling the PWA.

---

## 4. Analytics (Optional)

If anonymous analytics are enabled in the future, they will only include aggregated, non-identifiable data, such as:
- Number of visits
- Pages viewed
- Device type (desktop/mobile)

**We will never use analytics to identify individuals or link any usage data to personal identities.**

## 5. External Links

**Dose Doctor** includes links to trusted harm-reduction organizations and resources (such as DanceSafe, Erowid, PsychonautWiki, and SAMHSA).

**We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites.**

Please review their respective privacy policies before interacting with them.
