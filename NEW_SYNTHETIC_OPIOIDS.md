# New Synthetic Opioids to Add

## Critical Designer Opioids Currently Circulating

### 1. Metonitazene
- Nitazene series, ~100x morphine potency
- Found in pressed pills and heroin
- Multiple overdose deaths 2020-2024
- Emergency scheduled Schedule I (US)

### 2. Etonitazene  
- Original nitazene from 1950s research
- Extremely potent (~1000-1500x morphine)
- Reappeared in illicit markets 2020+
- Schedule I

### 3. Protonitazene
- Another nitazene variant
- ~20-50x morphine potency
- Found in counterfeit pills
- Linked to deaths across US/Europe

### 4. Brorphine
- Novel synthetic opioid (2018)
- Caustic, damages tissue
- Found in pressed pills
- Multiple deaths reported

These will be added to reagents.json with full testing data.
